Task 2 (ALTER TABLE - RENAME): The owner decides "inventory" sounds too boring. Rename the table to games.
Task 3 (ALTER TABLE - MODIFY): You realize the price column doesn't account for massive collector's items. Change the data type or constraint of the price column so it can support larger numbers or ensure it cannot be empty (e.g., NOT NULL).
